LLM Override Resolver

When enabled, borderline OVERRIDE_REQUIRED cases are reviewed by llama3.1:8b via Ollama instead of being auto-rejected. Adds ~3–5 min to each run — best suited for overnight/scheduled scrapes.

Always On Keyword Classifier

Source Performance Notes

Aggregators

Indeed

Highest yield. Returns full max_results reliably. Aggregates from company career pages. Expect 3-4x more results than LinkedIn.

LinkedIn

Throttles unauthenticated access heavily. Typically returns 30-50% of max_results. Best for finding roles at specific companies.

Glassdoor

Moderate volume. Unique advantage: often includes salary ranges. May require proxy for consistent access.

Google Jobs

Meta-aggregator pulling from multiple boards. Good for catching listings others miss. May have duplicates with Indeed.

Direct ATS & Community

Greenhouse + Ashby

Direct ATS API scraping from 100+ curated tech companies (Anthropic, Stripe, Cloudflare, Notion, Figma...). Highest quality listings. ~1000-2000 jobs per run.

Wellfound

Startup-focused (ex-AngelList). Uses TLS impersonation for anti-bot bypass. Great for Series A-C companies. ~100-200 jobs per run.

Climatebase

Climate and sustainability tech roles. Niche but growing sector. ~50-100 jobs per run.

Hacker News

Monthly "Who is Hiring" threads via Algolia API. Community-sourced, often has unlisted startup roles. ~200-400 jobs from last 2 months.
